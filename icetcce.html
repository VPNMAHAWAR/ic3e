About the conference The Conference IC3E-2018

As a part of Diamond Jubilee Celebrations, J. K. Institute of Applied Physics and Technology (Department of Electronics and Communication), University of Allahabad is organizing an International Conference on Emerging Trends in Communication, Computing and Electronics (IC3E-2018). The conference aims to bring together Researchers, Scientists, Engineers, Students, and Academicians belonging to different fields of Communication, Computing, and Electronics. The conference will also provide an intellectual platform to present their original work and discuss new and innovative ideas through original research papers involving theoretical as well as practical aspects of different fields of engineering research. The conference will include invited keynote lectures and paper presentations from both academia and industries to encourage young researchers in the field of science and technology for innovative and high quality research.


Original papers are invited in the following areas of research but not limited to
  • Communication Systems
  • Optical Fiber Communication
  • Information Technology
  • Antenna and Wave Propagation
  • Signal and Image Processing
  • Video Processing and Computer Vision
  • Human Computer Interaction
  • Artificial Intelligence & Evolutionary Computing
  • Machine Learning
  • Big Data Analysis
  • Biometrics
  • Electronics Circuits and Devices
  • VLSI Design
  • Embedded Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Modern Digital Systems
  • Real Time Systems
  • Wireless Sensor Networks
  • Cloud Computing
  • Robotics
  • Micro & Nano Electronics
  • Photonics
  • Mobile & Optical Networks
  • Network Security
  • Remote Sensing and GIS
  • Software Engineering
  • E-learning
